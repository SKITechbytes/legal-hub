# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how **Investment Calculator – SIP, Loan & EMI** ("we", "our", or "app") handles user information.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Financial account details  

All calculations (SIP, EMI, investment planning) are performed locally on your device and are not stored or transmitted to any server.

---

## 2. Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect:

- Device information (model, OS version)  
- Advertising ID  
- IP address (approximate location)  
- App usage data  

This is used to:

- Show relevant ads  
- Improve ad performance  

Learn more:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

---

## 3. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all age groups.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected directly, risk is minimal.

However, third-party services (like AdMob) may collect limited data.

---

## 4. Data Security

We do not store user data. All calculations are processed locally on your device.

We use:

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.
